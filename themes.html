Themes

Major themes




Theme Explanation
Loss and Grief The protagonist Ellery Stevens struggles with the
loss of her younger sister and the later grief that follows.
Hope and Redemption In the face of all her depression, Ellery's journey
to hope is a central theme. She struggles with suicidal thoughts and
her realization of the importance of life and connection.
Family Dynamics The relationship between Ellery and her family especially her
is a significant part of the story.
Mental Health The book discusses the serious topic of mental health
including the influence of grief and the importance of seeking help.
It discusses the struggles of young adults dealing with mental health.

What is the author trying to teach?

The author is trying to inform readers about mental health awareness
and its healing process through Ellery Stevens' point of view. It reflects
on the complexities of life after loss and the importance of finding joy.

My Personal Takeaway

My personal takeaway is that, it's easier to find ways to cope with
grief rather than shoving it inside. Love can have an impact on a person's
life when it comes to making difficult decisions.
