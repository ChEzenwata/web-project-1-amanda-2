Characters

Main Characters

The main characters of Teach Me To Forget include:

Ellery Stevens: protagonist who struggles with her grief and plans to end her life
but her plans are complicated by a gun malfunction.

Colter Sawyer: a boy in Ellery's English class who becomes intent on
stopping her from taking her own life and is determined to save her.

Supporting Characters

The supporting characters include:
Dean: Ellery's childhood friand who has also survived a suicide attempt.
