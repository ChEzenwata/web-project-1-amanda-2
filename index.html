Teach Me To Forget by Erica M. Chapman

Book title and author

Teach Me To Forget is a novel about a teenage girl learning how to
cope with grief and painful memories after a big loss. While she struggles
with her emotions and relationships, she discovers that healing does not mean
forgetting the past, but learning to move forward with it. Written by Erica M. Chapman.

Who might enjoy this?

Teach Me To Forget is a book that resonates with readers
who admire know-it-all characters, short sentences and humor.
It's perfect for fans of All The Bright Places and Holding Up The Universe.
